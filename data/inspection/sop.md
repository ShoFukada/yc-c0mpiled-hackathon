# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP): Nike Air Max SC Authentication

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Document ID | AUTH-AMSC-003 |
| Target Model | Nike Air Max SC (Grey/Charcoal/White) |
| Objective | Visual verification of key manufacturing variances between authentic and counterfeit pairs |

---

## Inspection Point 1: Lateral Swoosh & Eyestay Overlays

**Focus Area:** The lateral Swoosh logo and the dark grey synthetic leather panels around the laces (eyestays).

### ✅ Authentic (Real) Characteristics

- The Swoosh logo features a subtle cream/light grey pebbled leather texture with a natural matte finish.
- The Swoosh shape tapers cleanly toward the front tip, ending in a sharp, slender point.
- The dark grey eyestay panels have a smooth texture with clean, even single stitching along the edges.
- The wavy-cut panel below the laces features distinctive small indentations (resembling punch-hole marks).

### 🚨 Counterfeit (Fake) Patterns

- The Swoosh surface appears smooth and shiny, resembling plastic rather than textured leather. It lacks the characteristic pebbled grain entirely.
- The stitching around the Swoosh is crooked, unevenly spaced, or inconsistent in thread tension.
- The Swoosh shape is distorted — the front tip looks thicker, blunter, and clumsy instead of tapering to a clean point.
- Overall the logo looks cheap and poorly manufactured, with a visibly lower material quality.

---

## Inspection Point 2: Air Unit & Midsole Construction

**Focus Area:** The lateral heel section of the midsole, specifically the structure around the window exposing the Air unit.

### ✅ Authentic (Real) Characteristics

- The Air window does not have a plastic "cassette" or painted border; it is cleanly carved directly into the white foam midsole with no visible border line.
- The cross-section of the white foam surrounding the window is smooth and even, with no burrs or crumbling.
- The Air bubble is fully transparent and clear, allowing the internal support pillars to be seen sharply.
- The support pillars visible inside the clear Air unit are thick and visually distinct.

### 🚨 Counterfeit (Fake) Patterns

- A thin, faint border is visible around the Air window, slightly darker than the surrounding foam — authentic pairs have no such border.
- The foam cutout around the window is slightly uneven or asymmetric, indicating lower molding quality (jagged, crushed, or imprecise edges).
- The Air bubble appears slightly hazy or cloudy, reducing the clarity of the internal structure. The support pillars may look blurred.
- Incorrectly copies designs from models like the Air Max 90, placing unnecessary painted lines or plastic parts around the window.

---

## Inspection Point 3: Toe Box Mesh & Mudguard

**Focus Area:** The front mesh material and the dark grey mudguard wrapping from the toe to the lateral side.

### ✅ Authentic (Real) Characteristics

- The toe box utilizes a finely and tightly woven grey mesh material with small, uniform holes.
- The mesh has a soft, premium feel with consistent weave density across the entire toe area.
- The dark grey mudguard follows a smooth curve from the toe to the midfoot, featuring neat single stitching with even spacing along the top edge.
- The toe silhouette rises with a natural, gentle curve from the outsole toward the tip.

### 🚨 Counterfeit (Fake) Patterns

- The mesh weave is less dense than authentic, with holes appearing noticeably larger than normal.
- The mesh texture looks rougher and more synthetic, as if made from cheaper material — it may appear stiff rather than soft.
- The mudguard stitching has minor irregularities such as slightly uneven spacing in certain sections, or fraying.
- The panel cutting is too large, causing the entire toe to look awkwardly bloated or chunky.
